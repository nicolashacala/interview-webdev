8Trust: The company

8Trust offers consultancy and web development service. The team is a mix of developers and managers so they do not only offer a website but also advise concerning your e-project. Therefore, their customers are mainly entrepreneurs and start-ups. As mentioned above, they do not only offer a website as they also provide maintenance and aim to think long term of your project. It is then easily understandable that their relationship with their customers relies, as suggests the 8 of 8Trust, on an infinite trust.
