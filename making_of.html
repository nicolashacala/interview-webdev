Making of

I was able to meet the employees of 8Trust thanks to my previous web development teacher. I first met the two owners of 8Trust who explained to me how they started and how their company's startegy is meeting their values. Then, they asked to one of their employee to have a chat with me about their usual day at 8Trust.

It was then really interesting to have the point of view both from the bosses and the employees.

The interviewed were:

  • Jean-Charles van der Bruggen (Owner, Analyst)
  • Alexis Renard (Owner, Manager)
  • Katrin Amadori (Web Developer)
